About the project

Football data, made readable.

MatchScope is a Flask analytics web app built around the 64 fixtures of the FIFA World Cup 2022.

It cleans raw match data, derives match outcomes and goal metrics, then presents the results through interactive-ready pages, summary statistics, and visualizations.
